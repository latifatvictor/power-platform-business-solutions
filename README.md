# 🚀 Power Platform Business Solutions

A collection of Power Apps and Power Automate solutions designed to digitise business processes, improve efficiency, and enhance operational visibility across Microsoft 365 environments.

---

## 🧠 Overview

This repository showcases real-world business applications built using the Microsoft Power Platform to solve operational challenges across different domains.

The solutions focus on:
- process automation  
- request management  
- workflow optimisation  
- improved tracking and visibility  

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Power Apps  
- Power Automate  
- Microsoft 365  
- SharePoint Online

## 📦 Solutions Included

### ✈️ International Travel Request & Approval System
Streamlines travel requests with structured submission, approval workflows, and status tracking.

---

### 🚗 Smart Parking Allocation & Management System
Digitises parking allocation with automated requests, approvals, and availability tracking.

---

### 📦 Logistics & Shipping Request Automation Solution
Simplifies shipping requests and improves coordination through automated workflows.

---

### 📚 IT Learning & Certification Tracking Platform
Tracks employee learning progress, certifications, and development goals.

---

### 🏢 Hybrid Workplace Desk Booking & Presence Management App
Enables desk booking and tracks workplace presence for hybrid working environments.

---

### 🧾 Digital Visitor Management & Check-in System
Modernises visitor check-in processes and improves tracking and security.
